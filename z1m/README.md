🏢 Z1:M (Enterprise Core Module)

Z1:M is the enterprise-grade backend system of G1:m, responsible for managing all administrative and financial operations across the platform.

While G1:m integrates human input, virtual environments, and robotics, Z1:M ensures that all actions are structured, tracked, and auditable in the digital domain.

👉 Role in G1:m:

Business logic and system state management
Financial and transaction tracking
Identity and access control
Operational data consistency
🧾 Z1:M Responsibilities
Workforce Management
Scheduling, attendance tracking, task allocation
Accounting & Finance
Double-entry bookkeeping, transaction records, reconciliation
Platform-wide Data Integration
Aggregation of operational data across all modules
Identity & Access Management
Role-based access control (RBAC)
Audit & Compliance
Immutable logs and traceable system activity
🧠 Z1:M Concept — Beyond Traditional RPA

Z1:M extends the concept of RPA (Robotic Process Automation).

Traditional RPA:

Automates user actions (UI-level automation)

Z1:M:
👉
Treats business processes as structured data and automates them at the system level

👉 This enables:

Consistent execution across systems
Reduced manual operations
Full auditability and traceability
